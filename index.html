About Me



I am currently a student at the University of Akron. I am studying computer science, programming, and web development. I have played soccer my entire life and travelled to over 20 states just to play in a few tournaments. My dad and I also like to golf frequently. In some of my other free time, I like to mess around with raspberry pis and come up with fun programs. Currently, I am trying to build an automated bartender using NodeJS, python, a couple DC motors, and a relay board.

Fun fact: my brother taught me how to solve a 3x3 rubik's cube on a six hour drive home from Maryland. Since then, I learned how to solve the 2x2, 4x4, 5x5, 6x6, and 10x10 cubes. My dad and I have only solved the 10x10 cube a couple times because it's too time consuming.
